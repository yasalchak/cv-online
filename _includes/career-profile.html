About Me

A researcher in biomedical engineering field. In 2022, I completed a doctoral degree with a research project focused on the analysis of electromagnetic field propagation inside the human body for the localisation of wireless medical implants. Core competencies: Wireless Body Area Networks; Wearable Technologies; RF propagation; Electromagnetic fields modelling; Biomedical instrumentation design and programming; Signal Analysis
